  • If you already have an account, use /app-login to sign in from a browser.

Common questions

  • I didn’t receive a password reset email. Check spam/junk, and confirm you used the same email as your mobile app account.
  • I can’t log in on the website. Ensure you are using the same email/password as in the BuddyBoard mobile app.
  • How do I delete my account? Email us from your account email address and include “Delete my account” in the subject.
